How It Works

Four steps from concept to conference-ready paper.

1

Define Your Research Topic

Enter a research idea or question. The pipeline automatically decomposes it into actionable hypotheses and identifies required experiments.

2

Automatic Literature Review

The system searches across academic databases, identifies seminal papers, and synthesizes the current state of research in your domain.

3

Execute Experiments

Code is generated and executed in an isolated Docker sandbox with GPU support. Results are analyzed and visualized automatically.

4

Receive Your Paper

A complete LaTeX paper is generated with verified citations, experiment charts, and proper academic structure. Ready for submission.
